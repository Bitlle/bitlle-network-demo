BITLLE NETWORK PROTOCOL (DEMO)
Demonstration of ERC20 Loyalty Token (LT) transfer without using ETH. Sender pays the commission in LT to the test aggregator Bitlle Network, which, using its proprietary ETH to pay for gas, publishes the transaction on the Ethereum network. In this Demo, the test aggregator spends 55,000 to 83,000 for gas to publish the transaction. With further optimization and batch transaction publication functions, the cost may be decreased by 30-50%.
